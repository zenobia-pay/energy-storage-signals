# The Interchange / Interchange Recharged
# Host: Originally Stephen Lacey & Shayle Kann, now Wood Mackenzie hosts
# Producer: Wood Mackenzie (formerly Greentech Media)
# Focus: Energy market analysis and clean tech

## Overview
The Interchange was the flagship podcast of Greentech Media, now produced by 
Wood Mackenzie after their acquisition. The show (now "Interchange Recharged") 
provides technical analysis of clean energy markets.

## History
- Original hosts: Stephen Lacey, Shayle Kann
- Greentech Media acquisition by Wood Mackenzie
- Rebrand to "Interchange Recharged"
- Current hosts: Sylvia Leyva Martinez, Bridget van Dorsten (interim)

## Episode Count
