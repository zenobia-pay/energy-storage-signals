# Volts Podcast
# Host: David Roberts (@drvolts)
# Platform: Substack (volts.wtf)
# Focus: Clean energy technology, politics, and policy

## Overview
Volts is a podcast and newsletter by energy journalist David Roberts, 
formerly of Vox and Grist. The podcast features deep-dive interviews
on clean energy topics with researchers, policymakers, and industry experts.

## Episode Count
400+ episodes (podcast + newsletter posts)

## Format
- Long-form interviews (60-90 minutes)
- Deep technical dives
- Policy analysis
- Audience: Energy professionals and informed enthusiasts

## Energy Storage Related Episodes (Selected Topics)

### Grid & Storage
- California's grid challenges and battery storage
- ERCOT and Texas battery boom
- Virtual power plants and distributed storage
- Vehicle-to-grid (V2G) technology

### Battery Technology
- Lithium-ion supply chains
- Long-duration energy storage technologies
- Battery recycling and circular economy
- Sodium-ion batteries

### Policy
- IRA implementation for storage
- State storage mandates (California, New York)
- FERC orders and grid integration
- Utility rate design and storage
