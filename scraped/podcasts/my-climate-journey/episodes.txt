# My Climate Journey (MCJ) Podcast
# Now Called: "Inevitable" by MCJ
# Source: https://mcj.vc/media/podcast
# Hosted by: Jason Jacobs

## Overview
My Climate Journey is a podcast hosted by Jason Jacobs, founder of MCJ Collective,
a venture fund and community focused on climate tech. The podcast features 
interviews with entrepreneurs, investors, policymakers, and thought leaders
working on climate solutions.

## Episode Count
500+ episodes as of 2026

## Format
- Long-form interviews (60-90 minutes typical)
- Focus on climate tech startups and investors
- Policy discussions
- Personal climate journeys
