# Catalyst Podcast
# Host: Shayle Kann
# Producer: Latitude Media (formerly Canary Media)
# Focus: Climate tech deep dives

## Overview
Catalyst is a podcast hosted by Shayle Kann, focusing on deep technical and 
business analysis of climate and clean energy technologies. The podcast 
features long-form conversations with entrepreneurs, investors, and experts.

## Episode Count
200+ episodes

## Format
- Long-form interviews (45-90 minutes)
- Technical depth on specific technologies
- Business model analysis
- Market dynamics

## Host
Shayle Kann
- Former Head of Research at Greentech Media
- Co-host of The Interchange podcast (with Stephen Lacey)
- Background in solar and energy market analysis
