# Rocky Mountain Institute (RMI)
# Source: https://rmi.org/about/
# Scraped: 2026-02-19

## Overview
Rocky Mountain Institute (RMI) is an independent, nonpartisan nonprofit founded in 1982 that transforms global energy systems through market-driven solutions to secure a prosperous, resilient, clean energy future for all.

## Mission
To transform the global energy system to secure a clean, prosperous, zero-carbon future for all.

## Founded
1982 by Amory Lovins

## Size
Over 700 employees worldwide (as of 2024)
