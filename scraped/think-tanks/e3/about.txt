# E3 - Energy and Environmental Economics
# Source: https://www.ethree.com/about/
# Founded: 1989
# Type: Energy Consulting Firm

## Overview
Energy + Environmental Economics (E3) is a consulting firm helping utilities,
regulators, policy makers, developers, and investors make strategic decisions
on clean energy implementation.

## Notable Work
E3 has been instrumental in California's clean energy transition,
supporting the state's renewable portfolio standard and grid planning
with high penetrations of solar, wind, and storage.

## Industry Influence
E3's analyses are frequently cited in regulatory proceedings and
policy debates. Their models (like RESOLVE and PATHWAYS) are used
by utilities and regulators for long-term planning.
