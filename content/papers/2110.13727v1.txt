Title: Novel Lithium-Sulfur Polymer Battery Operating at Moderate Temperature

Authors: Vittorio Marangon, Daniele Di Lecce, Luca Minnetti, Jusef Hassoun

DOI: 10.1002/celc.202101272

Abstract:
A safe lithium-sulfur (Li-S) battery employs a composite polymer electrolyte based on a poly(ethylene glycol) dimethyl ether (PEGDME) solid at room temperature. The electrolyte membrane enables a stable and reversible Li-S electrochemical process already at 50Â°C, with low resistance at the electrode/electrolyte interphase and fast Li+ transport.
