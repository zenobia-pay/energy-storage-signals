Abstract:
In this paper, we implement an advanced safety filter to smoothly limit the current of an inverter-based Battery Energy Storage System. The task involves finding suitable Control Barrier Function and Control Lyapunov Function via Sum-of-Squares optimization to certify the system's safety during grid transients.
